My Journey to Fredericton: Education, Career, and Family Life

Posted on May 31, 2025 by Kinga Gyeltshen

From Bhutan to Atlantic Canada

My journey brought me from Bhutan to Fredericton, New Brunswick, where I came to pursue a Master's program at the University of New Brunswick. As a research-based student in Electrical Engineering, I found both academic challenges and incredible opportunities in this welcoming Canadian city.

Academic Achievement and Career Transition

After successfully completing my program, I joined the workforce in the last quarter of 2022. Fredericton has proven to be an excellent place for professional growth in the engineering field, with its growing tech sector and research opportunities.

Family Life in Atlantic Canada

What makes Fredericton truly special is what it has offered to my family:

  • A safe, welcoming community to raise children
  • Beautiful natural surroundings perfect for family activities
  • Four distinct seasons that each bring their own charm
  • Excellent education system and healthcare
"The Atlantic Canadian lifestyle has given us the perfect balance between career opportunities and quality family time."
"The Atlantic Canadian lifestyle has given us the perfect balance between career opportunities and quality family time."

Exploring The Beautiful Region

From the vibrant festivals of Fredericton to the dramatic tides of the Bay of Fundy, our summers are filled with adventures that blend culture and nature. On land, we hike through Odell Park's lush trails and step into history at Kings Landing. By the ocean, we marvel at Hopewell Rocks' flowerpot formations and breathe in the salty air of Saint Andrews. Each destination—whether Fundy National Park's waterfalls or the soulful rhythms of the Harvest Jazz & Blues Festival—reveals why Atlantic Canada's beauty captivates our family year after year.

Why We Chose to Stay

Fredericton offers the perfect combination of:

  1. Career opportunities in engineering and technology
  2. Excellent education system for our children
  3. Affordable quality of life
  4. Natural beauty right at our doorstep
  5. Career opportunities: Growing tech and engineering sector with research opportunities
  6. Education: Excellent schools and universities for our children
  7. Quality of life: Affordable living with access to nature and amenities
  8. Community: Welcoming atmosphere for immigrants

As we continue our Canadian journey, we're grateful for the warm welcome we've received and look forward to many more years in this special part of the country.

About Me

About The Author

Electrical Engineer | UNB Researcher | Bhutanese-Canadian Explorer

Born in the Himalayan kingdom of Bhutan and now rooted in New Brunswick's vibrant landscapes, I bridge worlds through engineering and storytelling. After completing my Master's in Electrical Engineering at the University of New Brunswick, I transitioned into the Canadian workforce in late 2022—where I now solve technical challenges by day and chase coastal adventures with my family by weekend.

This blog chronicles:

  • 🔌 Engineering Insights: From thesis research to industry applications
  • 🌊 Atlantic Living: Why Fredericton stole our hearts
  • 🏔️ Cultural Threads: Blazing momos with maple syrup, and other Bhutan-Canada fusions

When not debugging circuits, you'll find me hiking Fundy's trails, introducing my kids to Bhutanese folklore, or perfecting the art of snowshoeing (a skill no Himalayan native expects to need!).
