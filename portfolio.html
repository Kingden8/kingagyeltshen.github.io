Artificial Intelligence (AI) has rapidly evolved from theoretical concepts to transformative real-world applications. Today, AI encompasses a diverse range of technologies, each with unique capabilities and use cases:

  • Machine Learning (ML): Powers predictive analytics, recommendation systems, and fraud detection by learning from data patterns.
  • Deep Learning: A subset of ML that enables breakthroughs in image recognition, natural language processing (NLP), and autonomous systems through neural networks.
  • Generative AI: Tools like ChatGPT create text, images, and media, revolutionizing content generation and creativity.
  • Computer Vision: Used in facial recognition, medical imaging, and self-driving cars to interpret visual data.
